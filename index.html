From Blueprint to Plate

A journey from architecture to culinary excellence

Chef Mario Pagán

Chef Mario Pagán is a renowned culinary innovator and passionate advocate for Puerto Rican cuisine. Though he initially pursued architecture in Miami, his passion led him to study at Johnson & Wales University and work under acclaimed chefs.

Born and raised in Puerto Rico, his early love for cooking was inspired by the women in his family. With over 20 years of experience, he has played a pivotal role in redefining and modernizing the island's culinary traditions through his "Nueva Mesa Latina" movement.

His cooking blends traditional Latin flavors with global influences. He champions sustainability and supports local agriculture, earning recognition from the James Beard Foundation and participating in their global initiatives.
